Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

The ultimate chewy chocolate chip cookie recipe. These cookies feature crisp golden edges with a perfectly soft, gooey center loaded with semi-sweet chocolate pockets.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt, flour, and chocolate chips. Mix until just combined.
  5. Drop large spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing them 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until edges are golden brown. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
