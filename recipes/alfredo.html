Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Description

Rich, creamy, and packed with garlic parmesan flavor. This quick dinner classic combines juicy pan-seared chicken breast with tender fettuccine noodles tossed in a scratch-made heavy cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, sliced
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1.5 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook fettuccine pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken slices with salt and pepper. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and sear chicken until golden and cooked through. Remove chicken from skillet.
  3. Lower skillet heat to medium. Melt butter, then add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a low simmer. Let simmer for 3-5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Turn off the heat. Whisk in Parmesan cheese slowly until completely melted and smooth.
  6. Toss the pasta into the sauce, top with sliced chicken, and serve hot.
