World's Best Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with a hearty meat sauce, layered with rich ricotta cheese, lasagna noodles, and tons of melted mozzarella. It takes a little effort, but it's completely worth it.

Perfect for Sunday family dinners or meal-prepping comforting leftovers for the week ahead.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for about 1.5 hours.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and a pinch of salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 50 minutes. Let rest 15 minutes before serving.
